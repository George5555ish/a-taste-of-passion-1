The Challenge

A Taste of Passion, an authentic Caribbean restaurant with two London locations, was struggling with inconsistent social media presence and low engagement. Despite serving exceptional food, their online visibility wasn't translating to foot traffic and brand awareness.

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive content-first strategy focusing on authentic storytelling, process-driven content, and strategic algorithm optimization to showcase their vibrant Caribbean culture and mouth-watering dishes.

Content Creation Process

We established a systematic approach to content creation, filming multiple pieces of content during each restaurant visit to maximize efficiency. Each piece of content was crafted with specific hooks, trending audio, and optimized captions to maximize reach and engagement.
